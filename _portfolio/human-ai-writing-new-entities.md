This project studies how AI-introduced new entities influence the creative
process in human-AI collaborative writing. I am interested in how new characters,
objects, settings, or concepts generated by AI reshape human imagination,
planning, ownership, and narrative direction.

## Research Question

How do AI-generated new entities change the dynamics of human creativity during
collaborative writing?
