<span class="project-name">RecGlass</span> is a reconfigurable smart-glasses
platform that empowers users to actively configure wearable sensors for
context-aware AI interaction, supporting more personalized robot control,
physiological awareness, and embodied self-agency.

## Research Question

How can reconfigurable wearable sensing help users actively shape AI interaction
around their own bodies, contexts, and intentions?
