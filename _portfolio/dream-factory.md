<span class="project-name">Dream Factory</span> is an LLM-agent-powered interactive educational script and
role-play system. Users role-play characters from textbook materials with LLM
agents to better understand the characters, stories, and historical contexts.

The project won the <strong>Regional 2nd Place Award</strong> in the <strong>AI
Innovation Competition hosted by Vivo</strong>.

## Research Question

How can LLM agents make textbook learning more imaginative, embodied, and
reflective through role-play?
