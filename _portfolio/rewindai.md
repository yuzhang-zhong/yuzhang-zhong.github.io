<span class="project-name">RewindAI</span> is an AI system that helps users build a personal digital twin and
simulate unchosen outcomes of past decisions. The goal is to support deeper
self-reflection by allowing people to revisit decision points, imagine
alternative trajectories, and reason about how their values and contexts shaped
their choices.

## Research Question

How can AI help people reflect on past decisions without simply optimizing for a
single "best" outcome?
